Criteria of Eligibility for Admission

Nationality: Candidates must be citizens of India.
Age: Applicants should have completed 17 years of age allowed.
Academic Requirements: Applicants should have passed Higher Secondary Examination of the Board of Higher Secondary Education of Kerala or an examination recognized equivalent thereto with at least 45% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM) put together. Rounding off of the percentage of marks to the nearest whole number is not permitted. Entrance Examination: Candidates should qualify in the Engineering Entrance Exam conducted by the Commissioner of Entrance Exams, Govt. of Kerala.
